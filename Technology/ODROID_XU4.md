### Important Aspects
- Processing
  - CPU: Samsung Exynos5422 Cortex™-A15 2Ghz and Cortex™-A7 Octa core CPUs
  - 3D Accelerator (~GPU): Mali-T628 MP6
  - Memory: 2GB LPDDR3
  - Storage: eMMC5.0 HS400 Flash Storage
  
- Power
  - Adapter: 5V @ 6A
    - 5.5mm/2.1mm barrel
  - Consumption: 10-20W
